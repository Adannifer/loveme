Participation Model

Each participant group required a different planning response, so the event was structured around role-based needs rather than a single generalized audience.

AttendeesNeeded activity previews, venue trust, easy entry points, free drinks/snacks, and reasons to move through the event.
CosplayersNeeded visibility, photo moments, social recognition, and a cosplay-friendly atmosphere.
NPCsNeeded station assignments, game rules, props, arrival coordination, and clear interaction formats.
VendorsNeeded arrival timing, booth placement, tables/chairs, and visibility in the event flow.
HostsNeeded stage schedule, timing, lighting, sound support, and live performance coordination.
StaffNeeded check-in, vendor support, NPC support, stage coordination, and on-site guidance workflows.
